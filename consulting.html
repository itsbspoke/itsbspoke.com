Architecture Automation

I've supported several applications with more than a hundred thousand users. The most critical mistake I've ever encountered is a belief that hosting on EC2 means you have the scalability of the cloud. Being ready to scale and grow capacity is one of the most fundamental technical challenges in today’s marketplace, and most engineers have only been trained to deliver software. Are you sure that your organization can handle 10x your current level of traffic? Even worse, are you sure it can't?

Likewise, while automated testing is becoming more popular, relatively few organizations practice continuous integration and delivery. Tech teams solve a host of problems doing their work, some percentage of which have absolutely nothing to do with business goals. During this time, your team is getting paid to do nothing for you. Management should be eliminating this time with extreme prejudice. Getting these technologies put in place can have a massive impact on team productivity.
